Error Tracking Made Simple.

How It Works

1
CONNECT YOUR ERRBIT APP

Connect your Errbit app in less than 30 seconds by using your host URL and API key.

2
WATCH YOUR UNRESOLVED COUNT

See the number of errors in your application. Your error counts refresh automatically.

3
TAKE IMMEDIATE ACTION

Click on your app in the menu bar to go directly into your application errors.

Features

Connect an unlimited amount of Errbit applications.

Easily see your unresolved error counts.

Connected applications link directly back to Errbit.

Unresolved error counts refresh automatically so you don't have to.

Errbit is a wonderful, open-source self-hosted error catcher that allows you to collect and manage errors generated by your web applications. Get Errbit.
